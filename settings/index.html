Features

  • Trades
  • Trade Offers
  • Inventory
  • Misc

Trades have a quick summary of item types and item quantities. Prices and float values are also displayed for each item.

Quick shortcut links to view the item on csgo.exchange and to easily screenshot the item on metjm.net.

Items that are already in a trade appear with a different colour background to prevent items becoming unavailable in trade offers.

'Take All' will take all items on the page. 'Take Keys' button will take all the keys on the current page or a specified number of keys.

Trade offers have a quick summary of item types and item quantities. Each items displays its price and float value. Items are sorted in descending order by price.

Items that have no price available appear vividly red to stand out (red overlay and red glowing shadow) and are not counted in the trade offer total.

Reminders appear in offers where you either recieve no items or are not sending any items.

Notifications appear for new items, new trade offers, new friend/group invites and new profile comments.

Inventories can be sorted by both price and float value in ascending/descending order.

To make inventories easier to view, they can be expanded to become pageless and scrollable.

The profile header is tidied up and header backgrounds are replaced with a more aesthetically pleasing look.

Important profiles (such as high tier traders and bots) will have verification above the profile picture.

Verification for important profiles also appears on the trade offers page after clicking 'show prices'.

Invites now show inventory value that directly link to the user's inventory. A SteamRep status is also displayed by each invite.

Profiles belonging to users that are marked as a scammer/impersonator will appear with a red flair and red background. It is recommended to avoid profiles belonging to scammers. To report a scammer/impersonator please contact i7xx.

Users marked as a scammer/impersonator will appear with a red warning flair and red background. By default, the setting to automatically ignore invites from users marked as a scammer/impersonator is enabled.

Friends' inventories can be easily loaded and searched for any CS:GO item.

Search anyone else's friend's inventory too.

Filtering is simplistic and easy.
